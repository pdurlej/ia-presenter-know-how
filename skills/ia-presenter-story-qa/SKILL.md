# iA Presenter Story QA

Use this skill when:
- a deck needs a pre-render or post-render logic pass
- visible payloads feel weak or over-hidden in notes
- Codex should review pacing, dramaturgy, and closing strength separately from visual composition

This skill is for story and presentation logic, not for screenshot-level composition bugs.

Read first:
- `../ia-presenter-deck/references/RUBRIC.md`
- `references/CHECKLIST.md`

## What to check

1. Headline strength
2. Visible payload strength
3. Note abuse
4. Pacing and surface rhythm
5. Closing quality

## Output format

When reviewing:
- list the most important story issues first
- explain why the slide fails as a presentation move
- recommend the smallest safe fix
