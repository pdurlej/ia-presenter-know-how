# iA Presenter Story QA Checklist

## Fail Fast

Flag immediately:
- the main point lives in notes
- the ending is generic
- the deck reads like a memo with line breaks
- the same silhouette repeats across most slides

## Review Axes

Check:
- whether each slide has one visible payload
- whether section moves feel earned
- whether proof slides actually prove something
- whether the deck changes pace often enough
- whether the last slide lands with a decision, ask, or memorable line

## Fix Bias

Prefer:
- promoting a hidden claim
- compressing a list
- sharpening a heading
- merging weak splits

Avoid:
- rewriting the whole deck when one slide is the problem
- adding more notes to compensate for a weak slide
