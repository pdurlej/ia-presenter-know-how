### What we changed first

Good case studies show sequence, not only outcomes.

	1. Unified the knowledge base
	2. Standardized response patterns
	3. Removed duplicate routing rules

### What changed after 8 weeks

	|---|---|---|

### What surprised us

Every strong case study needs one human surprise.

### What you can copy tomorrow

Portable lessons make the case usable.

	1. Start with one messy workflow
	2. Reduce tool switching before adding automation
	3. Measure trust, not just output
