# Image Plan: `01-ai-support-copilot-pitch.iapresenter`

> Late-stage sourcing plan for the reframe-led pitch deck.

This deck should stay mostly text-led.

The goal is not to decorate every slide. The goal is to introduce 3-4 image-led moments that:
- reset rhythm
- ground the argument in a real work context
- keep the deck from feeling like a wall of text

## Keep These Slides Text-Only

These are stronger without images:
- slide 1: cover
- slide 2: `How I work`
- slide 4: `The five pressures on SaaS in 2026`
- slide 8: `What we will prioritize`
- slide 9: `What we will stop rewarding`
- slide 10: `What I will watch every month`
- slide 11: close

## Recommended Image Slots

## Image Quality Rules

Reject images that feel:
- too futuristic
- too polished or ad-like
- obviously staged startup cliché
- dependent on tiny UI details that will crop badly
- branded in a way that breaks the synthetic company world

Prefer images that feel:
- operational
- human
- a bit tense
- believable inside a SaaS team or buyer workflow

## Working Rule

If one image makes the slide feel less precise, keep the slide text-only.
