# Introducing iA Presenter: The Story-Based Presentation App

> Reading time: 7 minutes

---

iA Presenter cuts creation time to a minimum. Its text-based interface means you can start with any existing piece of writing. The design engine automatically adjusts the design of your slides to your phone, PC, or projector aspect ratios.

---

## Our Starting Point

We looked at the presentation challenges that we've been dealing with. The struggle of getting started. Understanding the old-fashioned and time-consuming format of static slides.

## Great Presentations Tell Great Stories

When you first open iA Presenter, you'll notice a text-based interface. By default, the focus is not on what your audience is going to see. The focus when making a presentation is on what you are going to say.

**First, write your story. The visuals are added later.**

Ironically, most of the time, we've already worked out much of what we need to say in another text document. We don't need to start from scratch every time we make a presentation.

With iA Presenter's text based editor, you can start with any existing piece of writing. Copy and paste. You've saved time and avoided the pain of starting a presentation.

---

## Your Script: Why Hide from Your Audience?

Reading from a slide is the fastest way to lose someone's attention. On average, we read 250 WPM (Words per Minute) while we speak at about 150 WPM.

This is why in iA Presenter what you say is only visible to you.

Is the idea to read everything from the teleprompter? No. You can use the Presenter's view like karaoke subtitles.

---

## Visuals: Tension and Attention

It's boring to read from the slide. It's as boring to explain what everybody sees.

**Less Is More**

Strong headlines and great images reinforce what you're saying. Together, they're a visual hook where you hang an idea.

Not every image is worth 1000 words. Stock imagery can be outright insulting to your audience's intelligence.

---

## Design: Colorful, Dynamic, Typographic

iA Presenter comes with a palette of colorful templates with great care for typography. The default template changes the background color as you progress. It's a new type of presentation design that dynamically creates a scale of background colors.

---

## Fun Like Karaoke

It's much faster to create presentations with a text interface. We can devote more time to practice. Practice helps with our confidence and our delivery.
