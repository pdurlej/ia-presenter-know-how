# Share Your Presentation in One Click

> Reading time: 4 minutes

---

Sharing a presentation should be quick. But it's slow. Viewing a presentation should be easy. But it's hard.

With Presenter you can share your presentation using just a link—that works in any browser on any device. No downloads, no apps, no plugins, no spying, no ads.

---

## How It Works

### Just upload, copy URL and send

That makes sharing presentations ideal for meetings and perfect for classrooms.

1. **Click Upload** - And the Presentation is up
2. **Send link** - Anywhere: phone, mail, social media

---

## What You Get

### Your shared presentations will always look great

Because slides dynamically adapt to any screen. Big or tall, small or flat. No more pinching to see small text.

### When you open a shared presentation

- The slides are shown at the top in a carousel
- Your speaker notes appear below
- Tap a slide to start the slide show
- Toggle speaker notes on and off by tapping a slide

### Or, use the handout option

View the presentation like a document. The carousel lets viewers follow the flow, step by step. Meanwhile, your notes at the bottom are prominent and easy to scan.

---

## Key Benefits

- **No Downloads:** Your audience just clicks a link
- **Responsive:** Works on any device, any screen size
- **Speaker Notes Included:** Toggle them on or off
- **Easy Updates:** If you have a typo you correct it and update - no new version emails
