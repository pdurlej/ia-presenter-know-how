# Anti-Patterns: Common Mistakes to Avoid

> **This document demonstrates common mistakes in iA Presenter. DO NOT use these patterns in your presentations.**

---

# ❌ WRONG: Using Spaces Instead of TABS

## The Mistake

This text should appear on the slide but I used spaces instead of TABS.

This will also not work because I didn't use actual TAB characters.

---

## ❌ WRONG: Mixed TABS and Spaces

Using spaces here

	Using TABS here

This creates inconsistent behavior.

---

# ❌ WRONG: Reading from Slides

## The Problem

I am now going to read everything that appears on this slide out loud to you instead of using my speaker notes. This is boring and you'll stop listening.

I am also reading this bullet point.

And this one too.

This is the #1 presentation killer.

---

## The Fix

Put detailed information in speaker notes (this area). Only show key points on the slide.

	Key point 1
	Key point 2
	Key point 3

---

# ❌ WRONG: Walls of Text

## Too Much Content

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat. Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate velit esse cillum dolore eu fugiat nulla pariatur. Excepteur sint occaecat cupidatat non proident, sunt in culpa qui officia deserunt mollit anim id est laborum.

---

## The Fix

Focus on one key idea per slide.

	- Simple concept
	- Easy to understand
	- Memorable

---

# ❌ WRONG: Too Many Bullet Points

## Excessive Lists

	- Point 1
	- Point 2
	- Point 3
	- Point 4
	- Point 5
	- Point 6
	- Point 7
	- Point 8
	- Point 9
	- Point 10

This is overwhelming and boring.

---

## The Fix

3-5 bullets maximum.

	- Main point 1
	- Main point 2
	- Main point 3

---

# ❌ WRONG: Generic Stock Images

## Cliché Visuals

	![Generic business handshake](/assets/stock-handshake.jpg)

This tells nothing about your actual content.

---

## The Fix

Use images that tell a story.

	![Specific, meaningful image](/assets/meaningful.jpg)

---

# ❌ WRONG: Missing Slide Breaks

## Run-on Content

This should be separate slide 1.

This should be separate slide 2.

This should be separate slide 3.

All jumbled together.

---

## The Fix

Use `---` to separate slides.

This is slide 1.

---

This is slide 2.

---

This is slide 3.

---

# ❌ WRONG: Confusing Side-by-Side

When side-by-side doesn't make sense.

This creates awkward layouts.

That doesn't belong with this.

---

## The Fix

Only use side-by-side when comparing related concepts.

Option A

This compares to option B.

---

# ❌ WRONG: No Hierarchy

Everything is a heading.

# This is a heading
## This is also a heading
### This is a heading too

No structure.

---

## The Fix

Use proper hierarchy.

# Main Title

## Slide Title

	Content that goes here.

---

# ✅ CORRECT: Good Example

## What Works

This demonstrates the correct approach:

Speaker notes explain the details.

	Key point visible
	Another point
	Third point

Clean, clear, effective.

---

# Summary Checklist

## Before Finalizing Your Presentation

Check for these anti-patterns:

	[ ] No spaces instead of TABS
	[ ] Not reading from slides
	[ ] Minimal text on each slide
	[ ] Fewer than 6 bullet points
	[ ] Meaningful images only
	[ ] Clear slide breaks (---)
	[ ] Logical side-by-side comparisons
	[ ] Proper heading hierarchy
	[ ] One idea per slide
